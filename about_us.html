About Us

We are a group of people trained in the field of AI and Analytics, and we hope that through this online platform we can educate and enhance people's knowledge of AI. This platform allows us to create virtual classes online and teach the required AI skills, and by completing the training service you will be equipped with skills applicable in the AI Analytics industry.
Our team consists of 2 main groups. Our first group would be the training personnel which consists of our team of trainers who come from different training institutions and are equipped with the knowledge and skillsets required for teaching AI Analytics. Our second group would be our consultation personnel team which consists of trained professionals who are already using their skills in the working industry. They will be able to provide information based on their experience in the field and assist people by improving and enhancing their knowledge and skills.
